If you have been in an accident, getting your vehicle restored to factory structural and safety specifications is your highest priority. At Second Generation Auto Body, we provide comprehensive collision repair services for drivers right here in Davie, FL, and across Broward County. 

From minor fender benders to major front-end and rear-end collisions, our team brings over 35 years of specialized mechanical and body craftsmanship to every vehicle that enters our shop.

### Structural and Cosmetic Restoration
A bad accident can compromise more than just your vehicle’s visual appeal. It can impact structural alignment, crumple zones, and safety components. We handle:
* **Frame Straightening & Realignment:** Ensuring your vehicle's structural chassis meets exact factory guidelines.
* **Panel Replacement & Repair:** Seamless installation of replacement quarter panels, doors, hoods, and bumpers.
* **Advanced Color Matching:** Using precision mixing technology to ensure your new parts match your original factory paint perfectly.

We understand that dealing with the aftermath of an accident is stressful. Our family-owned operation is built around transparent, honest assessments, ensuring you understand exactly what your vehicle needs to be safe and roadworthy again.
