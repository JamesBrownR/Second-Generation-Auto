A premium paint finish is all about the preparation. Without proper sanding, masking, and priming, even the highest-grade paint will fail over time under the intense Florida sun. At Second Generation Auto Body, we treat every single refinishing job with the level of care required for a high-end showroom floor.

Whether you are dealing with sun-faded clear coats on your roof and hood, looking to execute a complete color swap, or need custom paint matched to specialized body modifications, our shop is fully equipped to deliver flawless results.

### Refinishing Services We Offer
* **Complete Vehicle Color Changes:** Transform your car, truck, or SUV with a deep, glossy new tone.
* **Sun Damage & Clear Coat Repair:** Stripping back failed factory clear coats on hoods, roofs, and trunks, re-priming, and shooting fresh base and clear layers.
* **Specialized Finishes:** Expert application of metallics, pearls, and custom blackout packages for roofs, hood scoops, and trim pieces.
* **Meticulous Post-Paint Care:** Every custom paint job goes through a thorough post-bake cut and buffing process to eliminate orange peel and surface imperfections.

We take pride in our artistry, ensuring that when your car rolls out of our Davie facility, the depth and clarity of the paint finish speak for themselves.
