Restoring a classic, vintage, or muscle car requires an entirely different skill set than standard modern collision work. It demands patience, specialized toolsets, historical knowledge of vehicle assembly, and an eye for perfect body lines. 

At Second Generation Auto Body, we welcome project cars into our Davie shop. We love classic iron as much as you do, and we take deep pride in bringing historical pieces of automotive history back to absolute showroom status.

### Full-Scale Restoration Craftsmanship
Vintage projects frequently hide decades of wear, improper past repairs, and structural rust. Our shop systematically addresses these issues:
* **Rust Remediation & Cutting:** Cutting out contaminated sheet metal and welding in fresh metal to guarantee structural longevity.
* **Panel Realignment & Fabrication:** Adjusting body panels, doors, and trunks to achieve pristine, uniform gaps across the entire profile of the car.
* **Trim & Hardware Removal:** Doing restorations the correct way—completely stripping down trim, windows, and exterior hardware prior to block sanding and paint application.

We maintain open communication lines throughout the lifespan of your restoration project, documenting details as we transform your vehicle back into a timeless masterpiece.
