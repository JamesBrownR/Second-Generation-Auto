Navigating the insurance claim process after an accident can often feel more difficult than the repair itself. Insurance companies frequently push vehicle owners toward high-volume, corporate collision networks that prioritize speed over meticulous craftsmanship. 

As an independent, family-owned collision repair shop in Davie, FL, Second Generation Auto Body works directly for you, not the insurance conglomerate. We accept and work with all major automotive insurance providers.

### Carriers We Partner With Daily
* State Farm
* GEICO
* Progressive
* Allstate
* USAA
* Liberty Mutual

### How We Handle the Process For You
You do not have to act as the middleman between your repair technician and an insurance adjuster. Once you bring your vehicle to our Oakes Rd location, the process is streamlined:
1. **Provide Your Claim Number:** Give us your basic claim details from your carrier.
2. **We Handle the Adjuster:** We coordinate directly with the insurance adjuster assigned to your case, presenting a comprehensive, honest line-item breakdown of the actual structural and cosmetic work required.
3. **Supplement Management:** If we uncover hidden structural damage after teardown, we handle the supplemental claim documentation directly with your carrier so your repairs aren't delayed.
