Our Story

About Second Generation Family Owned Since Day One

Over 35 years ago, a passion for doing collision work the right way turned into a shop. That shop is still here, still family run, and still doing the work the same way it always has.

Where We Came From

Built on Craftsmanship, Not Volume

Second Generation Auto Body started as a commitment to doing collision repair the right way. Not the fast way. Not the cheap way. The right way. That meant caring about every panel, every paint line, every car that came through the door regardless of what it was worth on paper.

The name says it all. This is a shop built to be passed down, not sold off. Tony built it with the intention that his son would one day work alongside him, and that is exactly what happened. When you bring your car here, you are dealing with people who have a reason to stand behind their work beyond the next transaction.

We are based in Davie, FL and serve all of Broward County. We work with all major insurance companies and take on everything from everyday collision repairs to full vehicle restorations. If you want honest work done well, this is the shop.

How We Work

What You Can Expect from This Shop

Honest Estimates

Tony will tell you what your car actually needs and what it does not need. If a repair costs more than it is worth, he will tell you that too. Several customers have noted he talked them out of work that was unnecessary.

Clear Communication

You will know where your car is at every stage of the job. No chasing someone down for an update. No surprises when you come to pick it up.

Done When Promised

We give you a timeframe and we stick to it. If something changes, you hear from us before you show up expecting your car to be ready.

Quality That Holds Up

Proper prep, quality materials, and a cut and buff on every full paint job. Cars leave this shop looking better than they came in. That is the standard, not the exception.

Insurance Handled

We work directly with all major carriers. If your adjuster pushes back on a repair, we handle that conversation. You should not have to fight for the work your car needs.

You Talk to the People Doing the Work

No service writers, no layers of staff between you and the shop. When you call, Tony picks up. When you have a question, you get an answer from the person who knows the car.

The Name Behind the Shop

Why It's Called Second Generation

Tony started this shop more than 35 years ago. He built it the way most good things get built: slowly, doing right by people, and letting the work speak for itself. Over time, his son came in alongside him and that is where the name comes from.

Second Generation is not just a name. It is the reason this shop operates the way it does. When a business is meant to be handed down, you do not cut corners on the work or on the customer. Everything has to be done in a way you can be proud of, because the next person carrying the name will have to live with it.

That is how we approach every car that comes through the door, whether it is a daily driver with a fender bender or a classic somebody has been saving for years.
